After delivering Luke, Leia, C-3PO, and R2-D2 to the Rebel Alliance, Han and Chewbacca receive a payment for their services and prepare to leave. Luke asks Han to stay and help the Rebels attack the Death Star, but he refuses, not wanting to get involved. Han has a change of heart and returns to save Luke's life during the final battle, ultimately enabling Luke to destroy the Death Star. Leia presents Han with a medal of honor alongside Luke and Chewbacca.
